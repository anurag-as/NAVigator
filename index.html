How to download your CAS from CAMS
  1. Go to camsonline.com → Statements → Consolidated Account Statement
  2. Under Statement Type, select Detailed (includes transaction listing)
  3. Under Period, select Specific Period and choose your full investment date range
  4. Under Folio Listing, select Transacted folios and folios with balance
  5. Enter your email and PAN, then click Submit — the PDF will arrive in your inbox
